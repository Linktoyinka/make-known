Pink Pearl Foundation

Empowering Women with Health Education and Support

Goals

  • Hosted successful online events with renowned professionals to raise awareness.
  • Raised funds to support community members in need of health services.
  • Promoted various events organized by the foundation to reach a wider audience.
  • Recognized and appreciated community members who actively promoted the foundation's message.
