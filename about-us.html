Meet Micah, our marketing strategist. He is a skilled problem solver and strategic thinker who approaches his work with a high level of professionalism and a commitment to quality service. His areas of expertise include campaign creation, digital marketing, account management, community retention, relationship brand building, and event planning. With experience working across a range of industries and markets, Micah has served clients ranging from major brands to local SMBs.

Notably, Micah played a leadership role in the rebranding of premier cool, successfully integrating the brand to connect with a younger audience. He holds a Bachelor of Science in Management Information Systems from Covenant University and a post-graduate diploma in Digital Marketing Communications from St. Lawrence College in Canada. He advanced in his career from social media coordinator to account manager. Micah has led campaigns for globally recognized brands such as Maybelline, Dano Milk, Molfix, and Hennessey, overseeing comprehensive media and advertising plans, monitoring campaigns, and developing insightful reports.

As a true digital nomad, Micah enjoys both the creative and technical aspects of marketing his passion allows him to excel in media planning and buying, advertising, marketing strategy, and social media marketing. Micah is able to leverage his diverse skillset to help his clients achieve their marketing goals with efficiency and excellence.
